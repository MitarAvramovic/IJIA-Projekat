Coffee Types

Espresso

A rich and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foam.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.
