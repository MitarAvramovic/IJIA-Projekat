Espresso

A rich and concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk, often topped with foam.

Cappuccino

A classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and milk foam.

Mocha

Mocha coffee blends rich coffee with chocolate flavors, tracing its origins to Yemen's historic port of Mocha.

Iced coffee

Iced coffee is a refreshing beverage made by brewing coffee and serving it chilled, often over ice, with milk or sweeteners.

Affogato

Affogato is an Italian dessert that combines a scoop of gelato or ice cream with a shot of hot espresso poured over it.

Black coffee

Black coffee is a pure, unsweetened coffee brew without milk or sugar, known for its bold flavor and simplicity.

Macchiato

A macchiato is an espresso "stained" with a small amount of milk or foam, offering a bold coffee flavor with a hint of creaminess.
