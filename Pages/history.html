History of Coffee

Coffee's origins trace back to the ancient coffee forests of Ethiopia. Legend has it that a goat herder named Kaldi discovered coffee when his goats became energized after eating berries from a certain tree.

From Ethiopia, coffee spread to Yemen, where it became integral to Sufi rituals. By the 15th century, coffeehouses emerged in the Middle East, and the drink quickly gained global popularity.
